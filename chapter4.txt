In the distant future, a renowned linguist is unexpectedly contacted by a group of enigmatic extraterrestrial beings. These aliens possess a language that transcends human understanding, allowing them to communicate complex ideas and emotions directly into the minds of others. As the linguist deciphers their language, she discovers that the aliens are seeking her help to resolve a profound moral dilemma that has divided their species for eons. This quest takes her on an interstellar journey, where she navigates the intricacies of interspecies ethics and discovers the interconnectedness of all sentient life. The narrative explores the boundaries of communication, the nature of ethical decision-making, and the potential unity of diverse civilizations.
